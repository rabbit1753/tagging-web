' that is a pity , ' returned the young man politely , but in his heart he rejoiced , for he wished to be alone . leaving the house , he entered a grove of trees which stood behind , and took the box from his pocket . he raised the lid , and out flew the three little birds . ' good master , what shall we do for thee ? ' asked they , and he answered , ' bring me the finest horse that ever was seen , and the grandest dress , and glass shoes . ' ' they are here , master , ' said the birds , and so they were , and never had the young man seen anything so splendid .
