" so sir we 's daughter was married to old dschang . but even after the wedding the latter did not give up his work as a gardener . he spaded the field and sold vegetables as usual , and his wife had to fetch water and build the kitchen fire herself . but she did her work without false shame and , though her relatives reproached her , she continued to do so .
