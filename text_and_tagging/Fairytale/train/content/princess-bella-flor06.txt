at first he was only a servant , who carried the king the water for his bath , and saw that his bed was made in a particular fashion . but he did his duties so well that his master soon took notice of him , and in a short time he rose to be a gentleman of the bedchamber . now , when this happened the younger brother had spent all the money he had inherited , and did not know how to make any for himself . he then bethought him of the king 's favourite , and went whining to the palace to beg that his brother , whom he had so ill - used , would give him his protection , and find him a place . the elder , who was always ready to help everyone spoke to the king on his behalf , and the next day the young man took up is work at court .
