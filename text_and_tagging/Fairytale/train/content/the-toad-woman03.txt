" then , " she said , " your first child ( meaning the dog ) will come and find you out . " these directions the young woman followed just as they had been given to her , and in a short time she heard her son , now grown up , going out to hunt , with his dog , calling out to him , " peewaubik -- spirit - iron -- twee ! twee !
