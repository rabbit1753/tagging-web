after a time the father came , together with a - sung , and made kung a present of a hundred ounces of gold . then the youth took kung and his wife by the hand , and told them to close their eyes . as soon as they did so off they went through the air like a storm - wind . all kung could notice was that the gale roared about his ears . when some time had passed the youth cried : " now we have arrived ! " kung opened his eyes and saw his old home , and then he knew that his friend was not of human kind .
