then old mrs . rabbit took a basket and her umbrella , to the baker 's . she bought a loaf of brown bread and five currant buns . flopsy , mopsy , and cottontail , who were good little bunnies , went down the lane to gather blackberries ; but peter , who was very naughty , ran straight away to mr . mcgregor 's garden and squeezed under the gate ! first he ate some lettuces and some french beans ; and then he ate some radishes ; and then , feeling rather sick , he went to look for some parsley .
