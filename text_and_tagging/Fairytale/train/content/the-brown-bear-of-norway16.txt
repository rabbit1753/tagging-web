she went into the lodge , and asked the woodman and his wife to take her into their service . they were not willing at first ; but she told them she would ask no wages , and would give them diamonds , and pearls , and silk stuffs , and gold thread whenever they wished for them , and then they agreed to let her stay . it was n't long till she heard how a young prince , that was just arrived , was living in the palace of the young mistress . he seldom stirred abroad , and every one that saw him remarked how silent and sorrowful he went about , like a person that was searching for some lost thing .
