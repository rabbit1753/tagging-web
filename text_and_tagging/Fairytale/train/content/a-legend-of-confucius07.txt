once , when the wicked emperor tsin schi huang had conquered all the other kingdoms , and was traveling through the entire empire , he came to the homeland of confucius . and he found his grave . and , finding his grave , he wished to have it opened and see what was in it . all his officials advised him not to do so , but he would not listen to them . so a passage was dug into the grave , and in its main chamber they found a coffin , whose wood appeared to be quite fresh . when struck it sounded like metal .
