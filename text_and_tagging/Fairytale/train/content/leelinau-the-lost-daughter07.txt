" he turned suddenly where she sat in the circle , pensively regarding the crooked ear which she held in her hand , and exclaimed : " leelinau , the old man is thine ! " laughter rung merrily through the corn - field , but leelinau , casting down upon the ground the crooked ear of maize , walked pensively away . the next morning the eldest son of a neighboring chief called at her father 's lodge . he was quite advanced in years ; but he enjoyed such renown in battle , and his name was so famous in the hunt , that the parents accepted him as a suitor for their daughter .
