" look at me , " said the top to the ball . " what do you say now ? shall we be engaged to each other ? we should suit so well ; you spring , and i dance . no one could be happier than we should be . " " indeed ! do you think so ? perhaps you do not know that my father and mother were morocco slippers , and that i have a spanish cork in my body . " " yes ; but i am made of mahogany , " said the top . " the major himself turned me . he has a turning lathe of his own , and it is a great amusement to him . "
