" do you see the castle yonder ? " said the king . their eyes grew as large as saucers and they looked . yes , indeed , they saw the castle . " who has dared to build such a castle on my ground ? " the courtiers bowed and scraped , but did not know . so the king sent for his soldiers . they came tramping in and presented arms . " send out all my soldiers and horsemen , " said the king , " tear down the castle instantly , hang whoever built it , and see to this at once . "
