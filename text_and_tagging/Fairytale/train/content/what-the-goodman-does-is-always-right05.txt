accordingly the exchange was made . when the matter was settled the peasant might have turned back , for he had done the business he came to do . but having made up his mind to go to the fair , he determined to do so , if only to have a look at it . so on he went to the town with his cow . leading the animal , he strode on sturdily , and , after a short time , overtook a man who was driving a sheep . it was a good fat sheep , with a fine fleece on its back .
