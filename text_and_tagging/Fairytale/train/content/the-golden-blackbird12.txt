two or three days later the master said to him : ' the porcelain maiden is here : but , though she is as lovely as the dawn , she is so wicked that she scratches everyone that approaches her . try if she will accept your services . ' when the youth entered the room where she was , the golden blackbird broke forth into a joyful song , and the porcelain maiden sang too , and jumped for joy . ' good gracious ! ' cried the master . ' the porcelain maiden and the golden blackbird know you too ? ' ' yes , ' replied the youth , ' and the porcelain maiden can tell you the whole truth , if she only will . '
