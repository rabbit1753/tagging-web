' what dost thou here ? ' she asked , laying her hand on the bowl . as she spoke she too stuck fast , and was not able to utter a word . then thunder was heard and a veil of darkness descended upon them . the castle vanished and they with it . when kieva , the wife of pryderi , found that neither her husband nor his mother returned to her , she was in such sorrow that she cared not whether she lived or died . manawyddan was grieved also in his heart , and said to her : ' it is not fitting that we should stay here . he have lost our dogs and can not get food . let us go into england -- it is easier for us to live there . ' so they set forth .
