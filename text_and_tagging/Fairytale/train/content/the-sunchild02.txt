then letiko went straight home , and said to her mother : ' while i was gathering herbs a fine tall gentleman came to me and charged me to tell you that you should remember what you promised him . ' when the woman heard that she was sore afraid , and immediately shut all the doors and windows of the house , stopped up all the chinks and holes , and kept letiko hidden away , that the sunball should not come and take her away . but she forgot to close up the keyhole , and through it the sunball sent a ray into the house , which took hold of the little girl and carried her away to him .
