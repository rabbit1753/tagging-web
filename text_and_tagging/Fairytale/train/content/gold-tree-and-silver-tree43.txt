so , because of her wickedness , she was , as the good book says , caught in her own net . for she had made the wine so poisonous that , almost before she had swallowed it , she fell dead at the two princesses ' feet . no one was sorry for her . she really deserved her fate . they buried her hastily in a lonely piece of ground , and very soon everybody had forgotten all about her . as for princess gold - tree , she lived happily and peacefully with her husband and her friend for the remainder of her life .
