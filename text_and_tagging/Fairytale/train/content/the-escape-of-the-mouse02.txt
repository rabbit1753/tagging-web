then they set forth , but the news of their coming ran swifter still . rhiannon and kieva , wife of pryderi , made haste to prepare a feast for them . and manawyddan found that pryderi had spoken the truth concerning his mother , and asked if she would take him for her husband . right gladly did she consent , and without delay they were married , and rode away to the hunt , rhiannon and manawyddan , kieva and pryderi . they would not be parted from each other by night or by day , so great was the love between them . one day , when they were returned , they were sitting out in a green place . suddenly the crash of thunder struck loudly on their ears . a wall of mist fell between them , so that they were hidden one from the other . trembling they sat till the darkness fled and the light shone again upon them . in the place where they were wo nt to see cattle , and herds , and dwellings , they beheld neither house nor beast , nor man nor smoke . neither was any one remaining in the green place save these four only .
