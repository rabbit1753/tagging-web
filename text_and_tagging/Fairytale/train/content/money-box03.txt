upon this there was a great uproar . even the engravings , which hung in frames on the wall , turned round in their excitement . they showed that they had a wrong side to them , although they had not the least intention to expose themselves in this way , or to object to the game . it was late at night , but as the moon shone through the windows , they had light at a cheap rate . and as the game was now to begin , all were invited to take part in it , even the children 's wagon , which certainly belonged to the coarser playthings . " each has its own value , " said the wagon ; " we can not all be noblemen . there must be some to do the work . "
