on the eve of the day fixed for her marriage , leelinau decked herself in her best garments . she arranged her hair according to the fashion of her tribe , and put on all of her maiden ornaments in beautiful array . with a smile , she presented herself before her parents . " i am going , " she said , " to meet my little lover , the chieftain of the green plume , who is waiting for me at the spirit grove . " her face was radiant with joy , and the parents , taking what she had said as her own fanciful way of expressing acquiescence in their plans , wished her good fortune in the happy meeting .
