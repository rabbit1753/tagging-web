the brave wunzh felt that his strength of body was even less than on the day before , but the courage of his mind seemed to grow . observing this , and how wunzh put his whole heart in the struggle , the stranger again spoke to him in the words he used before , adding : " to - morrow will be your last trial . be strong , my friend , for this is the only way in which you can overcome me and obtain the boon you seek . " the light which shone after him as he left wunzh was brighter than before . on the third day he came again and renewed the struggle . very faint in body was poor wunzh , but he was stronger at heart than ever , and determined to prevail now or perish . he put forth his utmost powers , and after a contest more severe than either of the others , the stranger ceased his efforts , and declared himself conquered . for the first time he entered wunzh 's little fasting - lodge . sitting down beside the youth , he began to deliver his instructions to him and to inform him in what manner he should proceed to take advantage of his victory .
