on the fifth day of the fifth month the festival of the dragon junk is held along the yangtze - kiang . a dragon is hollowed out of wood , painted with an armor of scales , and adorned with gold and bright colors . a carved red railing surrounds this ship , and its sails and flags are made of silks and brocade . the after part of the vessel is called the dragon 's tail . it rises ten feet above the water , and a board which floats in the water is tied to it by means of a cloth .
